### Entry Hazards
- **`Hazard`** enum: `STEALTH_ROCK`
- `state.hazards: { ally: Hazard[], opp: Hazard[] }`
- `applyHazard()` / `clearHazards()` on BattleEngine
- Stealth Rock: 12.5% max HP damage on switch-in (no type effectiveness in initial release)

### Status Tick Damage
- `processStatusTick()` called during `advanceTurn()`
- Burn: 1/16 max HP/turn, halves ATK
- Poison: 1/8 max HP/turn
- Paralysis: 25% skip chance, halves SPE
- Cursed: 1/8 max HP/turn + 25% max HP recoil to attacker if faints
- Life Drained: 1/8 max HP/turn, source heals equal amount
- Permanent statuses (BURN, POISON, PARALYSIS) do not expire
- Items like Flame Orb, Toxic Orb apply status at end of turn
